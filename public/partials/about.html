Overview

App allows you to add a score to your google calendar events and then visualize the data creating a record of your productivity.

Technologies used

  • Main: AngularJS, Google Calendar API, Google Charts API for Data Vizualization
  • Design: Google Material Design, AngularMaterial, Animate.css, icon8.com for those cool icons.
  • Database, Storage, Hosting: Firebase

    • Inspiration

    I wanted to learn more about Angularjs. I also wanted to increase my productivity and track my progress on some of my self improvement goals. like going to the gym. I usually use google calendar to schedule my days and boost my productivity but I never usually do everything on the calendar. So the week goes by and I really haven't been nearly as productive as my google calendar would infer see pic below. This web app serves to track how productive I really am and to motivate me to be more productive.
